🏆 Aqua Mouse Hall of Fame

These mice lived in our store and are now sold. Each one is a piece of liquid mouse history.

Fluvi Mouse
Zaragoza, Spain · Expo 2008
Fluvi was the official mascot of the Zaragoza 2008 World Expo, themed around water and sustainability. This mouse was a promotional item featuring a small Fluvi figurine floating in blue liquid. Only a few thousand were ever produced.
